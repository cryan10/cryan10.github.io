Belly Dance


Schedule a Class

BELLY DANCE is the oldest style of dance in the world. It is identified by swaying hips, undulating torso, and articulated isolations. Movements in the dance include curving patterns, undulations, thrusts, lifts, locks, drops, and shaking body movements. Dancers focus on isolated movements of individual parts of the body. Arms and hands move fluidly, like serpents or ribbons in the air.

Each class begins with a warm-up, followed by a step-by-step exploration of basic movements that build into the foundation of all Belly Dance styles.

Olivia

Olivia has been a professional belly dancer in the Cleveland scene for 9 years. She was a primary dancer for Arab night at Kan Zaman, Club 360 and Diamond Lounge Jack Casino, and has been so lucky to travel around the USA to perform and study under the biggest names in Arab Dance and music. Her goal is to promote the deep history and beauty of the ancient Arab culture through music and dance.
