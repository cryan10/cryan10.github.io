We love our students here at Be Studios, especially our monthly members. Their passion for pole is such an inspiration to us as teachers! So each month we’ll be spotlighting a new student!


OCTOBER’S MEMBER SPOTLIGHT IS TORI



How long have you been doing pole?

I’ve been doing pole for 1 year


What was your first class like?

I was equally nervous and excited for my first class because I wasn’t really sure what to expect. I didn’t think I would be able to do anything. Luckily I come from a dance background and I was able to do more than I thought and I had a lot of fun.


What is your favorite trick?

My favorite trick to do is Peter Pan.


What has been the biggest challenge since starting pole?

The biggest challenge for me was trusting my own body. I used to second guess everything before trying it and then not being sure of myself. Now I try and just go for it without over thinking. It’s still a work in progress.


What is your dream trick to land?

I can’t wait until I’m able to do a Jade.


What is favorite part of pole fitness?

My favorite part of pole fitness is getting to dance and work out along side so many other talented and hard working people. The support from everyone is just amazing. I get to work on building physical strength and confidence in every class.


What advice would you give to new pole students?

Just have fun and trust yourself. You never know if you’ll be able to do it if you never try.
