Shakti Flow

At our Cleveland location


Schedule a Class

SHAKTI FLOW blends yoga, dance and functional fitness to create an invigorating practice. It is a way of life inspired by movement. On the surface, a Shakti Flow class is full of high intensity cardio intervals, fun improv dance sets mixed with creative vinyasa flow, body weight strength training and breath work while loud motivating music infiltrates the room.

Below what we can physically see, these movements are cleansing and opening our chakras, building solid foundations, and nudging us to step out of our comfort zones. In turn we re-emerge more confident, vibrant and aligned with our divine feminine energy. Shakti means power or empowerment, which is what our practice inspires within us. This practice will challenge you no matter your experience in yoga or fitness, and it is guaranteed to awaken the inner goddess that resides within us all.

Janice Spicuzza

Janice received her Shakti Flow certification in the summer of 2017. She was always a distance runner and yoga enthusiast. She found Shakti Flow to be a great cross-training practice for any fitness activity. It combines cardio with strength and flexibility by fusing dance sequences and fitness moves with yoga poses. Shakti Flow's high intensity intervals have helped Janice build endurance for pole dance routines better than running ever could. Whether you are a pole dancer, aerial artist or are just looking for a new workout that is fun and motivating, this practice incorporates everything you need to get fit fast!

Jessica Saal

Jessica has been a lifelong enthusiast of group fitness and is an avid soccer player, runner, and snowboarder. She has been practicing Shakti Flow for two years and is now a certified instructor in the practice. In a class with Jessica, you’ll hear loud, upbeat, empowering music, while combining elements of yoga, functional fitness, dance, and meditation. Jessica’s positive energy and spirit vibrate throughout the class which help students connect to the music and movement for a sweat-drenched workout.
