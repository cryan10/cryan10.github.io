Our Story

Be Studios opened in 200? under the direction of Christina Anderson. Her mission was to create a safe space for women to feel free to express themselves. The studio was the first pole fitness studio in Cleveland, OH and, since then, has grown into a fitness boutique. Be Studios mission is to... grabbed filler text from Fleur page... foster positive transformation in women via physical and interpersonal strength through body awareness, self-expression and feminine empowerment. Our vision is for women to feel comfortable and confident in their expression; physically own what they say; be unapologetic about who they are; and free themselves to self-actualize.


Be Studios is looking for passionate, driven men and women to expand its mission. If you are interested in opening your own Be Studios location, please contact us at info@lovebestudios.com with "New Be Studios Location" in the email's subject line.

Christina Anderson


My name is Christina and I am the founder of Fleur de Femme. I started this program based on what it has also given me. The guts of this program is what I was able to reflect and naturally gather from all my experiences. I was a nude model for years for art classes and private photographers at a time when I felt extremely uncomfortable in my body (all through the 90's early 2000’s). I was a performance artist for years using my body, improvising mostly, and a conceptual artist. Some examples of my art includes hanging myself from a meat hook; wrapping my body in corsets and hanging in a cocoon of them; undressing from the corset and biting into a beet, which to me symbolized the heart; and making a cast of my body and then a wax mold to hang on the hooks.

I started stripping when I was 28. I never stepped foot in a club like that prior. I named myself Bliss. I danced my heart out on stage but then I had to learn to interact with men. I had no confidence with this task and that was the only way to make money. The other girls trained me and taught me the moves. I watched and learned. I stumbled and felt super shy and awkward. Yes, tequilla became my close friend who opened the dark doors.

In a short amount of time, I felt wild, and free. I felt a sense of compassion for myself and most men I danced for and talked with. It was a strange dance between the primal naughty and the exchange of energy from one person to the other. I felt free to be vain or celebrate my body and the body of other dancers. I felt free to be "into" myself. It was not all narcissistic! I was profoundly beautiful and times in which it had nothing to do with the men. Just the self. Strippers like to watch themselves and see other watching them - there is a deep psychology in that and about the life in the club.

I wanted to bring this experience to other women, but in a safe place without the dangers of working in a club. I created this program using all of the experiences and techniques that shaped who I am today.


Cristina Romanello


Cristina Romanello reveals the inner mysteries of the commonplace, allowing her audience to ponder the beauty of the scenes we so often take for granted. Cristina succeeds in evoking our sense of wonder by unveiling the usual to reveal the exceptional.

Her passion demanded that she put the art before the course- she developed her style in her hometown, Cleveland, with only a disposable camera and the wet streets as her studio. After studying photography professionally, she found the last piece she needed to create a world of beauty we might never have glimpsed if we hadn't looked twice.

Wanida Joy


My name is Wanida and I’m the lead coach for Fleur de Femme. The moment that Christina told me what she wanted to do and accomplish with this program, I instantly fell in love. It encompassed everything that I loved about pole at Be Studios and aspects of my personal life that have helped me heal my inner wounds and become my truest self.

Teaching all the levels of Fleur the first time was such an amazing healing and learning experience for me. I learned so much about myself and grew in ways that I couldn’t even imagine would happen. I had been teaching yoga and pole, but this was so much more. This was full of heart, of passion, of soul. We cried. We laughed. We really allowed ourselves to be vulnerable and formed a bond I thought could never be broken.

As I’ve continued to lead women through this experience I’ve learned how to really hold space for them to let them release their fears, their doubts, and to really let there walls come down with no fear of judgement. What’s most beautiful and most important is Fleur is to have that safe space to explore who you really are without the outside world telling you who you should be or that you’re not good enough. This really allows you to find, what’s my voice and what is someone else’s. And once you’re able to find your own voice, to find the confidence to share it with the world even if they don’t understand it. Because ultimately, true sexiness is authentic confidence in who you are.

Maya Soul


Maya Soul’s pole style is a stripped flow. She is focused on freedom of expression, simplicity, strength and flow. She hopes that her students are able to find the courage to accept themselves and the freedom to express themselves. She began doing pole because of the shame that she felt. She was sick of being ashamed of her body and her sensuality. Pole has helped her heal and regain her freedom and confidence. She has been doing pole for over 3 years and has been teaching off and on for 2 years.

Outside of pole Maya Soul is interested in psychology, art therapy, finance, and being a badass. “Dim the lights. Divine speaks in flow.”
