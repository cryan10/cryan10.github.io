Photography


In our pole or aerial photoshoot sessions you will embrace your unique sensuality and natural inner beauty. With various use of props and fabrics, light and shadows, you will create a set of images that speak to you and your soul. Each of us is unique and carry with us our own beauty. Be your own kind of beautiful!


We only book photoshoots in the studio. No pole, aerial or model experience necessary for shoots. There are many beautiful poses you can do on and around the apparatus. If you would like to do a shoot outside of the studio, you can visit Cristina Romanello's website to contact her directly.


Please allow 2-3 weeks for editing. Each image is carefully selected and edited to fit the emotion of the photo. There will be color and black/white photos at the discretion of the artist to fit the mood of the overall shoot.


Please call the studio at (216) 227-9710 to book your photoshoot.

Bio

Cristina Romanello

Cristina Romanello reveals the inner mysteries of the commonplace, allowing her audience to ponder the beauty of scenes we so often take for granted. Cristina succeeds in evoking our sense of wonder by unveiling the usual to reveal the exceptional.

Her passion demanded that she put the art before the course- she developed her style in her hometown, Cleveland, with only a disposable camera and the wet streets as her studio. After studying photography professionally, she found the last piece she needed to create a world of beauty we might never have glimpsed if we hadn't looked twice.


"There is beauty all around us, and having an instrument to capture and document these moments is remarkable."
