Pole Parties

Celebrate a night out at Be!


Have a party to celebrate a...

  • Girls' night out
  • Bachelorette party
  • Birthday
  • Any other special occasion

We offer parties 1-2 hours in length tailored to the desires of the host. Parties include introductory pole dancing tricks and techniques like sexy walk, floorwork and chair dance. You can come dressed up or casually comfortable. Heels and cameras are welcomed.

Guests are welcome to bring drinks of their choice. Light food is allowed for parties 1.5-2 hours in length. We supply cups, plates, silverware, etc. Everyone attending the party is responsible to pay.
