** GROUPON CUSTOMERS **

Please register for the first class that you wish to attend and select your Groupon option to complete checkout. Groupons MUST be purchased prior to your first visit. You do NOT need to enter a promo code to redeem the Groupon. Please bring your voucher with you to the studio (printed or Groupon app) to your first class. If your Groupon expires, you may use the “paid value” of your Groupon as class credit towards any class or package.


**LATE CANCELLATION POLICY**​

Due to our limited class size, Be Studios has a strict late cancellation policy. A $10 fee will automatically be charged for any no shows or cancellations that occur later than 24 hours prior to scheduled class time. This policy applies to all students, no exceptions. The late fee is charged in lieu of your class pass. You will not lose one of your passes if you cancel with less than 24 hour notice; you will only be charged the late fee.

If you are waitlisted for a class, the late cancellation fee still applies. If you are added to the class and can no longer attend, please give us a 24 hour notice to avoid the late cancellation fee. If you have not yet been added to the class, please make sure to remove yourself from the waitlist. Thank you for your cooperation!
