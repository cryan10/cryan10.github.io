Kids Aerial Parties



Looking for something new and unique to do for your kid’s next birthday party? Need a new idea for a team bonding experience for your sports team? Be Fitness offers aerial parties catered just for kids! Kids have the option to do aerial hammock, aerial silks, and aerial hoop. Aerial parties are for kids ages 6 and up.

Our kids aerial parties are perfect for…

  • Birthday celebrations
  • Girl Scout troops
  • Cheerleading squads
  • Gymnastics squads
  • Any other special occasion

We offer parties 1-1.5 hours in length tailored to the desires of the host. Parties include introductory aerial tricks and techniques. Please come dressed casually comfortable. In order to keep our silks and hammocks clean, food and drinks are not permitted for aerial parties. Cameras are welcomed, and parties include a group photo.

Our Instructor

Emily
Bio

Emily

Emily loves all aspects of aerial dance but, when it comes to choreography, she prefers to keep it slightly more aggressive. She likes fast paced, high silk climbing with dramatic drops. She loves aerial silks because of the endless possibilities that they provide to express yourself. You can be great at floor work and incorporate a lot of it in your routines. Or you can be awful at floor work and focus more on aerial tricks. Your dance style can be graceful or aggressive, or a mix of both. The creativity is endless!

Emily started doing aerial silks as a fun, easy way to work out because she hated going to the gym. She had no idea how much it would become a part of her life! She has been doing aerial silks since 2012 and teaching since 2015. She hopes that her students gain the tools they need to find their own style of dance, while also pushing their limits to conquer routines and transitions that they may not master right away.

Outside of aerial silks, Emily loves her pets and rescuing fur babies. She likes to ride her motorcycle and she also has three kids who take up most of her time. They spend their weekends camping, fishing, and boating. She has a bachelor’s degree in Biology from Cleveland State University and really just loves anything science related.
