Performances




Are you looking to enhance your next party or event? Our Be Fitness adult performance troupe, Orchid Body, offers extraordinary performances on pole, aerial silks, and aerial hoop (lyra). Experience art, movement, and flow with our stunning performers and exciting routines.

We are available for public events, corporate events, concerts, private parties, and more! We offer a variety of styles from family-friendly performances focusing on strength and acrobatics, to lyrical performances focusing on movement, and exotic performances for adults.

We offer solo or group performances that are personalized for your event. Professional photography is also available at an additional fee. Please call (216) 227-9710 or email us at info@befitnesscle.com for more information.
