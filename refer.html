Be Studios Referral Program



Refer your friends to Be Studios and receive additional benefits!

  • Refer 5 friends and get 1 class FREE when they attend their first class!
  • Refer 5 friends who sign up for a membership and get 1 month of your membership FREE!

*Referrals must be new students

Check Your Referrals

1.) Log in to your MindBody account. Under 'My Info' select ‘Account’.

2.) You will see your rewards at the bottom of the screen.

3.) Once you have earned 20 points, you can redeem those points for a single drop in!

The rewards will automatically be credited to your account when you check out.
